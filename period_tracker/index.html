Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Period Tracker & Calendar ("we," "us," or "our") handes information. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy, especially regarding sensitive health data, and ensuring a secure experience.

Note: This application currently does not contain any advertisements.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We respect your privacy. Core health and cycle data you enter into the application (such as period dates, symptoms, moods, and notes) is stored locally on your device by default, ensuring you have full control over your personal information.

3. Permissions

To provide a seamless tracking and reminder experience, the app requires the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
Notifications To send timely reminders about your upcoming period, ovulation, or pill schedules.
Alarms & Timers To ensure notifications and reminders are triggered accurately on time.
Media / Storage To allow you to attach images to your daily diary notes and manage personal backups.
Internet Access To facilitate anonymous analytics and error reporting, as well as handle in-app purchases.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services for app stability and basic usage analytics. We do not sell your personal health data to any third party.

  • Firebase Analytics: To understand general app usage trends and improve user experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To identify and fix technical issues and app crashes.
  • Google Play Services: Required for core Android OS functionalities.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your core health data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to any external servers. You can permanently delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the application from your device or clearing the app's data in your device's settings.

For automatically collected information via third-party services (like Firebase Analytics), data is largely aggregated and pseudonymous. We retain such information for up to 14 months. You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Application.

If you have provided us with any direct personal information (like an email address for support) and wish to have it deleted, please contact us at manhdung5a@gmail.com. We will process your request and delete your data within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

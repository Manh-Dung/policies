Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how PDF Reader & Scanner - OCR ("we," "us," or "our") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience.

Note: This application currently does not contain any advertisements.

2. Permissions and Data Usage

To provide document scanning and management services, the app requires the following permissions. All file processing is done locally on your device.

Permission Purpose
Camera To capture document images for scanning and OCR.
Storage To read, import, and save PDF files and images.
Manage Files To organize, rename, and manage your PDF library.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services for app stability and basic usage analytics:

  • Firebase Analytics: To understand how the app is used and improve performance.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To identify and fix technical issues and crashes.
  • Google Play Services: Required for core Android functionality.

4. Data Privacy & Security

All core functions (OCR, PDF generation) are performed locally on your device. We do not upload your documents or personal scans to any servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
